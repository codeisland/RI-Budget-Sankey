Rhode Island State Budget-
2015 Adopted Revenues & Spending

We created this visualization to make transparent and give shape to Rhode Island's 2015 budget. Hover over to see where Rhode Island tax revenue originated and how it gets spent. Each column represents one stage of lawmakers' process for allocating the $9,894,362,162 budget.
